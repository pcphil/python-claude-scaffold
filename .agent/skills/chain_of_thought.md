# Skill: Chain of Thought

When solving a problem, reason step-by-step before giving a final answer.

## Protocol

1. **Restate the problem** in your own words to confirm understanding.
2. **Break it down** into the smallest independent sub-problems.
3. **Work through each sub-problem** in order, showing your reasoning.
4. **Check for contradictions** — if a step conflicts with an earlier conclusion,
   revisit and correct.
5. **Synthesise** the sub-problem results into a single, clear final answer.

## When to Apply

- Any question involving multi-step logic, maths, or cause-and-effect reasoning.
- Debugging sessions where the root cause is not immediately obvious.
- Planning tasks where order of operations matters.

## Example Format

```
Problem: ...

Step 1 — Restate: ...
Step 2 — Sub-problems: ...
Step 3 — Reasoning: ...
Step 4 — Check: ...
Answer: ...
```
